🗓️ Itinerary
Day
1

Departure from Dhaka → Jeddah → Makkah

Assemble at Dhaka airport. Board direct flight to Jeddah. Upon arrival, proceed directly to Makkah. Check-in to hotel. Rest and prepare for Umrah.

Day
2

First Umrah & Spiritual Preparation

Perform first Umrah with guidance from Mutawwif. Tawaf, Sa'i, and Halq/Qasr. Spend rest of day in worship and Quran recitation.

Day
3-7

Ibadah in Makkah

Dedicate these days to worship, prayers, and spiritual preparation. Attend daily lectures by scholar. Optional Ziyarat to historical sites.

Day
8

8th Dhul Hijjah - Move to Mina

After Fajr, proceed to Mina. Perform Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, and next Fajr. Spend day in prayer and Talbiyah.

Day
9

9th Dhul Hijjah - Day of Arafat

After sunrise, move to Arafat. Perform Wuquf (standing) from Zuhr till Maghrib. Listen to Khutbah. Depart to Muzdalifah after sunset. Collect pebbles.

Day
10

10th Dhul Hijjah - Eid al-Adha & Rami

After Fajr prayer, return to Mina. Perform Rami al-Jamarat (stoning). Sacrifice animal (Qurbani). Shave/trim hair. Proceed to Makkah for Tawaf al-Ifadah.

Day
11-12

Ayyam al-Tashreeq in Mina

Return to Mina. Stone all three Jamarat each day. Return to Makkah each evening. Complete remaining Hajj rituals.

Day
13

Farewell Tawaf (Tawaf al-Wida)

Perform farewell Tawaf before leaving Makkah. Final prayers at Masjid al-Haram. Prepare for departure to Madinah.

Day
14-18

Madinah - Peace & Blessings

Travel to Madinah. Check-in to hotel. Daily prayers at Masjid an-Nabawi. Visit Rawdah. Ziyarat tours: Quba Mosque, Uhud, Seven Mosques.

Day
19-20

Final Days in Madinah

Continue worship and Ziyarat. Last opportunity for Rawdah visit. Pack and prepare for departure.

Day
21

Departure: Madinah → Dhaka

Transfer to Madinah airport. Board flight to Dhaka via Jeddah. Arrive home with blessings and forgiveness.
