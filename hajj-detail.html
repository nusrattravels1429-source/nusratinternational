📋 About This Package

A complete 21-day Hajj package with accommodation in both Makkah and Madinah. Fully guided by experienced Islamic scholars, our group takes care of every logistical detail — from airport pickups and visa processing to Mina tent arrangements and Arafat transportation — so that you can focus entirely on your spiritual journey.

মক্কা ও মদীনায় আবাসন সহ ২১ দিনের সম্পূর্ণ হজ প্যাকেজ। অভিজ্ঞ আলেমদের গাইডেন্সে সকল কার্যক্রম পরিচালিত — ভিসা, পরিবহন, মিনার তাঁবু সবকিছু আমরা সামলাই।

🗓️ Programme
Day
01

Departure from Dhaka

Group assembly at Hazrat Shahjalal Airport. Don Ihram, fly to Jeddah.

Day
02

Arrival in Makkah

Check-in hotel, perform Tawaf al-Qudum (welcome tawaf), rest and lectures.

Days
03–07

Makkah Ziyarat

Perform Umrah, guided tours of Masjid al-Haram, Jabal al-Nour, Jabal Thawr, and preparation lectures for Hajj.

Days
08–09

Mina & Arafat

Proceed to Mina on 8 Dhul-Hijja. Stand at Arafat on 9th — the peak of Hajj. Overnight at Muzdalifah.

Day
10

Eid al-Adha

Rami al-Jamarat (stone-throwing), sacrifice, shave/cut hair, Tawaf al-Ifadah, Sa'i.

Days
11–13

Tashreeq Days

Continue Rami on 11, 12, 13 Dhul-Hijja. Return to Makkah. Final Tawaf al-Wida.

Days
14–20

Madinah Ziyarat

Travel to Madinah. Visit Masjid an-Nabawi, Raudhah, Masjid Quba, Masjid al-Qiblatayn, and Uhud.

Day
21

Return to Dhaka

Farewell, airport transfer, fly home — Alhamdulillah.
