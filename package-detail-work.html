📋 About This Package

Start a new chapter of your life in Malaysia with our comprehensive work visa package. We handle everything — from the very first document to handing you over to your employer in Kuala Lumpur. Job placements are in reputable factories, construction companies, plantation sectors, and service industries. Competitive salary, free employer-provided accommodation, and medical benefits are standard for our placements.

🗓️ Step-by-Step Process
01

Initial Consultation (Free)

Visit our Dhaka office. Meet our counselor, assess your qualifications, education and physical fitness. Choose the right job category and country employer.

02

Document Collection

Submit passport, NID, educational certificates, medical test (GAMCA approved), police clearance certificate and passport photos.

03

Visa Application Submission

We prepare and submit your work visa application to the Malaysian High Commission on your behalf. Timeline: 3–6 weeks.

04

Visa Approval & Work Permit

Receive your official work permit, employment confirmation letter, and employer contact details. All verified and authentic.

05

Pre-Departure Orientation

Attend our 2-day briefing: Malaysian laws, worker rights, safety norms, emergency contacts, banking setup, and cultural awareness.

06

Departure to Malaysia ✈️

Fly to Kuala Lumpur with our airport assistance. Employer pickup arranged at KLIA. We provide a local emergency contact for your first week.
