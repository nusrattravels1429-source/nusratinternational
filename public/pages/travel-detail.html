📋 About This Package

Experience the magic of Santorini with its iconic whitewashed houses, magical sunsets over the Aegean Sea, and charming villages. Our 7-day package includes accommodation in a traditional cave hotel, guided tours of Oia and Fira, wine tasting at local vineyards, and a sunset cruise around the caldera.

সান্তোরিনির আইকনিক সাদা বাড়ি, এজিয়ান সাগরের মনোমুগ্ধকর সূর্যাস্ত এবং charming গ্রামগুলো অভিজ্ঞতা নিন। ৭ দিনের প্যাকেজে ঐতিহ্যবাহী কেভ হোটেল, ওয়াইন টেস্টিং এবং সানসেট ক্রুজ অন্তর্ভুক্ত।

🗓️ 7-Day Itinerary
Day
01

Arrival in Santorini

Arrive at Santorini Airport, transfer to your cave hotel in Oia. Welcome dinner with sunset views.

Day
02

Explore Oia Village

Guided walking tour through Oia's narrow streets, visit the famous blue-domed churches and art galleries.

Day
03

Fira & Wine Tasting

Morning trip to Fira capital, afternoon wine tasting at Santo Wines Winery with caldera views.

Day
04

Sunset Caldera Cruise

5-hour sailing cruise around the volcanic caldera, swimming in hot springs, BBQ dinner on board.

Day
05

Akrotiri Archaeological Site

Visit the ancient Minoan city of Akrotiri, then relax at Red Beach and Perissa Black Beach.

Day
06

Free Day / Optional Tours

Free day to explore on your own or join optional tours: ATV rental, cooking class, or catamaran trip.

Day
07

Departure

Final Greek breakfast, souvenir shopping, transfer to airport for departure — Kali Taxidi!
