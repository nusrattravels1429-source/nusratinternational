📋 About This Program

Factory and construction work in Malaysia with a competitive salary package. Accommodation and flight ticket are provided by the employer, making this one of the most accessible overseas employment programs for Bangladeshi workers. Nusrat International handles all documentation, BOESL registration, medical check coordination, and embassy processing on your behalf.

কারখানা ও নির্মাণ কাজ, প্রতিযোগিতামূলক বেতন এবং নিয়োগকর্তা প্রদত্ত আবাসন ও বিমান টিকেট সহ মালয়েশিয়া কর্মসংস্থান। সকল কাগজপত্র ও প্রক্রিয়া আমরা পরিচালনা করি।

🗓️ Process Steps
Step
01

Application & Medical

Submit documents (passport, NID, photos). Complete medical examination at GAMCA-approved centre. Fingerprint registration.

Step
02

Visa Processing

Malaysia High Commission reviews application. Approval typically takes 4–8 weeks. Nusrat International tracks and follows up.

Step
03

BOESL Registration

Register with Bangladesh Overseas Employment and Services Ltd. Complete pre-departure orientation briefing.

Step
04

Contract Signing

Review and sign employment contract. Confirm job category, salary, working hours, and benefits with employer.

Step
05

Departure to Malaysia

Fly Dhaka → Kuala Lumpur. Employer representative picks you up at KL airport and transfers to workplace & accommodation.
