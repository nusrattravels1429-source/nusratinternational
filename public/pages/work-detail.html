📋 About This Program

Secure your future with our Malaysia Work Visa program. We offer placement in manufacturing, plantation, and construction sectors with competitive salary packages ranging from MYR 1,500 to 2,500 per month. Our comprehensive service includes visa processing, flight booking, accommodation arrangement, and 24/7 support throughout your contract period.

📝 Application Process
Step
01

Submit Application

Fill out the application form with your personal details, educational background, and work experience. Submit required documents including passport copy, photographs, and CV.

Step
02

Document Verification

Our team verifies all documents and submits your application to the Malaysian Immigration Department and Ministry of Home Affairs.

Step
03

Medical Examination

Complete medical check-up at approved FOMEMA clinic including chest X-ray, blood test, and general health screening.

Step
04

Visa Approval & Stamping

Upon approval, receive your eVISA and get it stamped in your passport. We also arrange pre-departure orientation session.

Step
05

Departure to Malaysia

Flight booking confirmed, airport pickup arranged in Malaysia. Welcome kit provided and you start your new job!
