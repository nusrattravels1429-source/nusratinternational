OUR STORY

A Legacy Built on Trust & Integrity

Nusrat International was founded with a singular vision — to provide Bangladeshi families with safe, legal, and dignified pathways to opportunity abroad.

What began as a humble travel agency in the commercial heart of Banani, Dhaka has grown into one of Bangladesh's most recognized names in two critical sectors: international manpower recruitment and travel services. Under the steady leadership of Alhaj Mohammad Zakir Hossain, the company has maintained an unwavering commitment to ethical practice, regulatory compliance, and genuine care for every client.

Today, Nusrat International operates two distinct but complementary divisions — Nusrat International for manpower recruiting and Nusrat Travels for aviation and tour services — both operating from the same Banani address and guided by the same values that have defined the organization from day one.

Our membership in BAIRA and ATAB, combined with IATA accreditation, reflects not just credentials on paper, but a lived commitment to operating with transparency, dignity, and care for every person who walks through our door.

"Our mission has always been simple: to serve our clients as if they were our own family — with honesty, care, and full accountability."
— Alhaj Mohammad Zakir Hossain, Proprietor

LEADERSHIP

The Man Behind
The Mission

Alhaj Mohammad Zakir Hossain is the founder and proprietor of both Nusrat International and Nusrat Travels. A veteran of Bangladesh's travel and recruitment industry, he has built both organizations on a foundation of regulatory compliance, professional integrity, and deep personal dedication to his clients.

His decision to pursue and maintain memberships in BAIRA and ATAB, alongside achieving IATA accreditation, reflects his belief that legitimacy and transparency are not optional — they are the foundation of any business that deals with people's livelihoods and dreams.

Having performed Hajj himself, Alhaj Hossain brings a personal and spiritual understanding to the sacred journeys his company facilitates every year for thousands of Bangladeshi pilgrims. This personal connection gives Nusrat International's pilgrimage services a sincerity that clients immediately feel and trust.

Under his leadership, the company has maintained a near-perfect visa approval record while placing thousands of workers in safe, legal, and dignified employment abroad — changing lives and supporting families across Bangladesh.

✦ OUR JOURNEY

Milestones That
Define Us

2010

Company Founded

Nusrat International established in Banani C/A, Dhaka with the founding mission of ethical manpower recruitment and professional travel services.

2012

BAIRA Registration

Formally registered with BAIRA, cementing our commitment to ethical overseas employment practices under national and international labor migration standards.

IATA Accreditation

Nusrat Travels received IATA accreditation — allowing direct ticketing for all major international airlines, a milestone signaling world-class professional trust.
